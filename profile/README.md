## The Philosophy

Everything we build starts here. Four words. One discipline.

| | |
|:---:|:---|
| **Q** &nbsp; Question | Progress begins with the right question. We challenge conventional wisdom, isolate the problems that matter, and seek the deeper understanding most teams skip past in their rush to ship. |
| **E** &nbsp; Explore | Curiosity, made operational. Research, experimentation, and patient discovery of methods that haven't been tried in our domain yet. |
| **E** &nbsp; Envision | Designing for what compounds. We work toward bold, specific futures — not just next-quarter targets. A clear vision tells you which trade-offs are temporary and which ones define the kind of company you become. |
| **T** &nbsp; Transform | Vision is decoration until it ships. Building things that reshape industries and improve lives — and then actually delivering them. |

## Our Companies

### [Qeet ID](https://id.qeet.in) — Identity & Access · Generally Available

> *The auth platform engineers wish they'd had.*

Qeet ID is our first venture. It started from a question every engineering team eventually asks: **why is authentication this expensive, this complicated, and this divided between "simple developer tools" and "real enterprise platforms"?**

Qeet ID closes that gap — developer simplicity on the surface, enterprise depth underneath.

**What it ships:**

- **SSO** — SAML 2.0 & OIDC across 50+ identity providers
- **MFA & Passkeys** — TOTP, SMS, email OTP, and WebAuthn for phishing-resistant sign-in
- **RBAC & ABAC** — fine-grained access control across products and tenants
- **SCIM 2.0** — automated user provisioning and lifecycle management
- **Multi-tenancy** — full tenant isolation with a single identity graph
- **Audit-ready logging** — SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR-aligned from the platform up

**SDKs:** TypeScript · Go · Python · Rust · React · Next.js · Mobile

**Free tier:** 5,000 MAUs, no credit card required → [id.qeet.in](https://id.qeet.in)
